About This Program

What is Young Entrepreneurship?

Young Entrepreneurship is Arc Innovate's business thinking program — where design thinking meets real commercial thinking. Over 8 weeks, children aged 7–10 learn to spot problems worth solving, develop a business concept around a real customer need, build a simple business model, and pitch their idea at Arc Innovate's Mini Market capstone event — where they design, make, price, and sell a real product at a stall. This is not theory. Students leave knowing they can go from idea to market, because they've done it.

Curriculum

What Happens Each Week

Week 1

Opportunity Spotting

Train your eye to see everyday problems that could become business ideas.

Week 2

Customer Empathy

Who is your customer? Interview real people to understand what they actually want.

Week 3

Idea Generation

Brainstorm 20+ business ideas. Learn to filter good ideas from great ones.

Week 4–5

Build Your Concept

Develop one idea: what is it, who is it for, why would they buy it, what does it cost?

Week 6

Pricing & Value

Simple financial thinking — cost, price, profit, and why value matters more than price.

Week 7

Pitch Prep

Build a simple pitch and practice presenting your business idea clearly and confidently.

Week 8

Mini Market

Design, make, price, and sell at Arc Innovate's Mini Market. The full cycle under real conditions.

Learning Outcomes

What Your Child Gains

💡

Entrepreneurial Mindset

Children learn to see problems as opportunities — a perspective shift that benefits them in school, creativity, and every future career path.

👥

Customer Empathy

Understanding what other people actually need (not what you assume) is one of the most valuable skills any person can develop.

💰

Financial Basics

Simple, age-appropriate understanding of revenue, costs, and profit — building financial literacy at exactly the right age.

🎤

Pitch Confidence

Every student pitches their business idea and sells at Mini Market — developing real-world communication and commercial confidence.

🔄

Full Cycle Experience

Going from insight to idea to product to sale in one term gives children an experience of the complete entrepreneurial journey — for real.

✨

Creative Confidence

Discovering that business ideas come from a process — not just talent — gives children the confidence that their ideas are worth developing.

Is This Right for My Child?

Who Thrives in This Class

✅ Great fit if your child...

Loves asking "why doesn't someone just make that?" Interested in money, business, or how companies work. Enjoys persuading, storytelling, or getting others excited about their ideas.

💡 No experience needed if...

No business experience needed — and no money required from parents for Mini Market. Arc Innovate provides everything. Children just need curiosity and enthusiasm for their idea.
