About This Program

What is Fashion Design?

Fashion Design at Arc Innovate is an 8-week creative program where kids aged 7–14 learn to think and work like fashion designers. Students explore the full design process — from inspiration research and mood boarding to fashion sketching, fabric selection, and building a mini collection. Rooted in design thinking, this class teaches children that fashion is not just about clothes — it's about identity, culture, and creative problem solving.

Curriculum

What Happens Each Week

Week 1

Fashion as Design

Explore fashion history and understand how clothing communicates identity and culture.

Week 2

Inspiration & Research

Build a mood board using images, textures, and colours to define a design direction.

Week 3–4

Fashion Sketching

Learn the basics of figure drawing and how to sketch clothing designs with confidence.

Week 5

Fabric & Materials

Explore how different materials feel, drape, and work for different design purposes.

Week 6

Collection Building

Design a 3-piece mini collection with a consistent theme, palette, and story.

Week 7–8

Portfolio & Showcase

Present your collection as a mini fashion portfolio at the end-of-term showcase.

Learning Outcomes

What Your Child Gains

✏️

Fashion Sketching

Children develop a foundational drawing skill specific to fashion — learning to communicate clothing ideas visually with confidence.

🎨

Visual & Aesthetic Thinking

Building mood boards and collections develops colour sense, composition, and the ability to see and communicate visual themes.

🌍

Cultural Awareness

Exploring fashion history and different design traditions builds appreciation for diversity and cultural expression.

📁

Portfolio Development

Students leave with a real mini design portfolio — a tangible record of their creative work and design thinking process.

💡

Creative Identity

Designing their own collection helps children discover and express their personal aesthetic — building a confident creative identity.

🗣️

Presentation Skills

Presenting their collection at showcase builds confidence, articulation, and the ability to explain creative decisions.

Is This Right for My Child?

Who Thrives in This Class

✅ Great fit if your child...

Loves clothes, style, or how people present themselves. Interested in art, drawing, or visual design. Enjoys expressing their personality through how things look.

💡 No experience needed if...

No drawing or sewing experience needed. This class focuses on the design thinking behind fashion — sketching is taught from scratch for complete beginners.
