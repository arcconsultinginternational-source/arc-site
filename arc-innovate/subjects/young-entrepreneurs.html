About This Program

What is Young Entrepreneurs?

Young Entrepreneurs is an 8-week program where kids aged 7–14 learn to think like founders. Using design thinking as their framework, students identify real customer problems, brainstorm business ideas, build a simple business model, and pitch their concept to a live audience on showcase day. No business background needed — just a curiosity about how things work and why people buy what they buy.

Curriculum

What Happens Each Week

Week 1

Opportunity Spotting

Train your eye to spot everyday problems that could become business ideas.

Week 2

Customer Empathy

Interview real people to understand what they actually want vs. what they say they want.

Week 3

Idea Generation

Brainstorm 20+ business ideas. Learn how to filter good ideas from great ones.

Week 4–5

Build Your Concept

Develop one idea into a full concept: product, customer, pricing, and value proposition.

Week 6

Basic Financials

Learn simple revenue models, costs, and what makes a business profitable.

Week 7–8

Pitch Day Prep

Build a pitch deck and practice presenting your business to a real audience.

Learning Outcomes

What Your Child Gains

💡

Entrepreneurial Mindset

Children learn to see problems as opportunities — a perspective shift that benefits them in school, creativity, and life.

🎯

Customer Empathy

Understanding what other people need (not just what you think they need) is one of the most valuable skills a young person can develop.

📊

Financial Basics

Simple, age-appropriate understanding of revenue, costs, and profit — building financial literacy early.

🎤

Pitch & Presentation

Every student pitches their business idea at showcase day, developing real-world confidence and communication skills.

🧩

Problem Solving

Students practice breaking big problems into smaller, solvable pieces — a transferable skill across every subject.

✨

Creative Confidence

Kids discover that ideas are not magic — they're a process. And that process is learnable by anyone.

Is This Right for My Child?

Who Thrives in This Class

✅ Great fit if your child...

Loves asking 'why?' and 'what if?'. Interested in money, business, or how companies work. Enjoys debating, storytelling, or convincing others of their ideas.

💡 No experience needed if...

Your child has never run a business — perfect. This is a beginner-friendly program designed to give children their first entrepreneurial experience in a safe, playful environment.
