About This Program

What is Young Innovator?

Young Innovator is Arc Innovate's flagship program — an 8-week journey where kids aged 7–14 learn the full design thinking process and bring their ideas to life through 3D printing. Students pick a real problem from their world, go through Empathize → Define → Ideate → Prototype → Test, and end the program with a physical 3D-printed prototype they've designed themselves. No coding or engineering experience needed — just curiosity and a willingness to try.

Curriculum

What Happens Each Week

Week 1–2

Empathize

Understand real problems through observation and user interviews.

Week 3

Define

Turn observations into a clear problem statement to solve.

Week 4–5

Ideate

Brainstorm 10+ solutions. Wild ideas welcome — this is the fun part.

Week 6

3D Design Intro

Learn to use beginner 3D design software to sketch your prototype.

Week 7

Prototype

Print your first physical prototype on the 3D printer.

Week 8

Test & Showcase

Test with real users and present at the end-of-term showcase.

Learning Outcomes

What Your Child Gains

🧠

Design Thinking Process

Children leave knowing exactly how to approach any problem using the 5-stage design thinking framework — a skill used by the world's top innovators.

🖨️

3D Printing Skills

Hands-on experience with 3D design software and operating a 3D printer — one of the most in-demand maker skills of the decade.

💬

Presentation Confidence

Every student presents their prototype at our end-of-term showcase, building real public speaking and communication skills.

🔍

Problem-Solving Mindset

Kids learn to research before jumping to solutions — a critical thinking habit that improves performance across all school subjects.

💪

Resilience & Iteration

When prototypes don't work, students learn to adapt and try again — building the resilience to handle failure positively.

🤝

Collaboration

Team-based projects develop the ability to listen, negotiate, and build on each other's ideas — essential 21st-century skills.

Is This Right for My Child?

Who Thrives in This Class

✅ Great fit if your child...

Loves building, making, or taking things apart. Enjoys creative challenges with no single right answer. Is curious about how things work and why problems exist.

💡 No experience needed if...

Your child has zero 3D printing experience — that's expected and totally fine. The program is designed for complete beginners and meets children where they are.
