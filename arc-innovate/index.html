Our programs

Three ways in, one way of thinking.

Every Arc Innovate program is built around the same core idea: children learn best when they're given real problems to solve, real materials to shape, and real room to create.

Weekend Creative Academy

Ages 4–10 · Weekends
Ages 4–6

Experimental Arts & Design

An early-years program that turns everyday materials into first design experiments — building colour sense, fine motor skill, and the confidence to try things out loud.

  • Sensory-led art and making sessions
  • Foundational design vocabulary
  • Confidence-building group activities
Ages 7–10

Design Lab

A project-based program where kids run a full design process — from spotting a problem to prototyping and presenting a solution, in teams.

  • Guided design thinking projects
  • Prototyping with real tools and materials
  • Presentation and pitch practice
Ages 7–14 · In-School

ECA at International Schools

A menu of after-school ECA electives delivered on-site at partner schools, each one a hands-on design thinking experience wrapped around a subject kids already love.

  • Fashion Design
  • Make Up Design
  • Young Innovator
  • Young Entrepreneur
  • Design Thinking
  • Creative Cooking
Ages 2.5–6 · Take-Home

Brain Play Kit

Guided, mission-based activity kits that bring design thinking and fine motor development home — for families who want the practice to continue between sessions.

  • Mission-based Brain Play Guides

  • Simple materials, ready in minutes

For schools, cafés & venues

An ECA program partner that fills the room.

Arc Innovate partners with international schools, kids' cafés, and event spaces to bring a ready-made creative enrichment program to their space — curriculum, facilitation, and materials included.

  • Turnkey delivery — curriculum, trained facilitators, and materials, ready to run in your space.
  • Shared enrollment — a simple revenue-share model for both school-sourced and Arc-sourced students.
  • Flexible formats — from a weekly ECA slot to a one-off birthday or showcase event.
About Arc Innovate

Built from over a decade in design education.

A

Teacher Anies

Founder, Arc Innovate and Arc Consulting

Arc Innovate is the children's creative enrichment arm of Arc — founded by Anies, a Bangkok-based educator and design teacher with over 12 years of experience in design thinking and education. With a background in Product Design and certification as an NLP Practitioner, she built Arc Innovate's curriculum around one belief: that design thinking is one of the most valuable habits of mind a child can develop, and it's best learned by doing.
