Ways to join in

Beyond the regular program.

On top of our core programs, Arc Innovate shows up for celebrations, holidays, and special moments.

🎂 Birthday Parties

Creative design workshops for birthday celebrations — kids work on a design challenge and leave with something they've made.

📅 Break Camps

Intensive creative weeks during school breaks — dive deep into design thinking, making, and building with peers.

🎪 Showcase Events

End-of-term celebrations where kids present what they've designed, built, and learned — inviting families to see the work.

Let's collaborate

Bring creative workshops to your space.

Arc Innovate works with schools, malls, event organisers, and kids' venues to bring hands-on design thinking workshops wherever children gather — we handle everything, you just open the doors.

  • 🏫 International Schools — Weekly ECA electives, holiday camps, and one-day design workshops delivered on-site by our team.
  • 🏬 Malls & Kids' Venues — Pop-up creative workshops and activity days that bring families through your doors.
  • 🎉 Events & Celebrations — Birthday party workshops, school fairs, corporate family days — any occasion that needs something memorable for kids.
  • ✅ We bring everything — Curriculum, trained facilitators, materials, and activities. You provide the space and the kids.
Why design thinking matters

What your child gains.

Experimental Arts & Design (Ages 4–7)

Building the foundation of a creative mind through sensory play and open-ended making.

  • Trust their instincts — experimenting without fear
  • Develop fine motor skills — through intentional making
  • Build confidence — celebrating what they create
  • Expand vocabulary — learning colours, textures, shapes

Design Programs (Ages 7–14)

Moving into structured problem-solving and the full design process.

  • Critical thinking — spotting problems and imagining solutions
  • Teamwork — collaborating and listening to others
  • Resilience — learning that failure is just feedback
  • Presentation confidence — pitching ideas to peers
