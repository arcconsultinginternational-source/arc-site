The Arc — Your Child's Learning Journey

One progression. Ages 4 to 14.

The Arc is Arc Innovate's 3-level curriculum architecture. Clean age boundaries, the same Innovator Skill Map at every level — only the depth and complexity changes as children grow.

Level 1

Maker

Ages 4–7

Sensory exploration, open-ended making, and "how might we fix this?" thinking. Curiosity-first, structure-light. Where the Design Thinking habit begins.
Level 2

Solver

Ages 7–10

Structured design projects with clear stages. Building, prototyping, 3D printing, and presenting real solutions to real problems. The heart of the Arc.
Level 3

Innovator

Ages 10–14

Full design thinking applied to complex real-world challenges — digital products, entrepreneurial ventures, and advanced maker projects.

How We Run

Four ways to learn with Arc Innovate.

We meet children where they are — in weekend studios, school classrooms, or holiday camps. Every format uses the same design thinking framework and closes with a showcase.

📅

Weekend Studio Classes

8-week terms, 2x per week. Small groups of max 10–12. The core Arc Innovate experience, with the full design thinking cycle per subject.

🏫

School ECA Programs

We bring the full Arc curriculum to your school as an after-school enrichment program. Everything provided — materials, facilitation, framework.

🏕️

Holiday Camps

Intensive week-long programs during school breaks. Full design challenge from day 1 to showcase on day 5. For kids who want to go deep fast.

🎂

Birthday Party Workshops

Half or full-day design challenges for groups of 8–16. Your child's birthday becomes a real innovation workshop. Memorable for everyone.

🎪

Showcase Events

Parent-facing exhibitions where students present their projects. Every term, every subject. The TELL chapter — communication as a ritual.

🛒

Mini Market Capstone

The Level 2 graduation event. Teams design, make, and sell real products at a market stall. The full innovator cycle under real conditions.

About Arc Innovate

Built by a designer. Run by an educator.

Arc Innovate was founded by Coach Anies — a product designer turned educator with 12+ years working with children at Bangkok international schools. After seeing firsthand how school curricula were preparing children for a world that no longer exists, she built Arc Innovate to fill the gap: a structured, design-thinking-led enrichment program that teaches children how to think, not just what to know.

Every program at Arc Innovate is rooted in the same framework used by the world's leading innovation companies — made accessible, playful, and genuinely age-appropriate for children from age 4.
